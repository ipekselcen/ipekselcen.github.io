Science Communication

I make complex research accessible through clear writing and thoughtful editing. With a PhD in chromatin biology and formal training in medical communications, I bring both scientific depth and communication clarity.

Philosophy

Great science communication starts with deep understanding. My background in molecular biology and bioinformatics gives me the fluency to engage with cutting-edge research.

But understanding isn't enough. The best writing makes complex concepts not just comprehensible, but compelling. I write for clarity, edit for precision, and always keep the reader in mind.
