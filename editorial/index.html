Science Communication

Making complex research accessible through clear writing and thoughtful editing

My Approach

Great science communication starts with deep understanding. My PhD in chromatin biology and hands-on experience with multi-omics analysis give me the technical fluency to engage with cutting-edge research across molecular biology, genetics, and computational biology.

But understanding isn't enough. The best writing makes complex concepts not just comprehensible, but compelling. I write for clarity, edit for precision, and always keep the reader in mind—whether they're a fellow scientist, a journal editor, or someone encountering the topic for the first time.
