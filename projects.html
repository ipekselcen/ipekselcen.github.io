🧬 RNA-seq Analysis: From Raw Reads to Biological Insight

Complete end-to-end pipeline with reproducible code

A comprehensive tutorial for RNA-seq analysis using public GEO data (GSE79018). Includes automated pipelines for quality control (FastQC/MultiQC), genome alignment (STAR), read quantification (featureCounts), differential expression analysis (DESeq2), and functional enrichment (clusterProfiler).

What you'll learn: Complete workflow from raw FASTQ files to publication-quality figures, best practices for reproducible research, and interpretation of RNA-seq results.

Research Projects

TET Enzymes in RNA Regulation

PhD Research | Multi-omics Analysis

Investigated the role of TET enzymes in establishing and maintaining gene expression programs in oligodendrocyte lineage cells. Integrated RNA-seq and hMeRIP-seq data to map regulatory landscapes and identify key regulators.

Developed reproducible R-based pipelines for multi-omics data integration, revealing novel connections between transcriptome and RNA modifications.

RNA-seq hMeRIP-seq Multi-omics Epigenetics R/Bioconductor

Histone Acetylation in Cell Proliferation

Published Research | RNA-seq Analysis

Investigated the role of histone H4 acetylation in neonatal and adult oligodendrocyte progenitor cells. Performed comprehensive RNA-seq analysis to identify differentially regulated genes and pathways.

Publication: Dansu, D.K., Selcen, I., et al. (2024). Journal of Cell Biology.
